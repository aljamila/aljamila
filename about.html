About Al Jamila Marble Working LLC

Jamila Marble is one of the largest companies in the UAE dealing in Natural Stones. We have a team of professionals with extensive knowledge and experience in Marble & Granite Industry and are actively engaged in imports of Marbles, supply, fabrication and installation in the Middle East region.

We undertake all Floorings, Kitchen Tops, Vanity Tops, Counter Tops, Wall Cladding, Crazy Paving, Tread & Risers, Cladding and Decorative Marble requirements. Our finished products whether external or internal demonstrates our professionalism, reliability and level of services we provide to our clients.  We create and install Marbles to the client’s exact specification within the specified time and allocated budget.

We have a clientele of highly reputed companies in the UAE both in private & Government sector. Our excellent association with such reputed clients over the years made us the Marble & Granite industry in the UAE.
